Critical Safeguards

Meeting Limits

  • Max duration: 3 hours
  • Max precision/meeting: 60 min
  • Max precision/day: 100 min
  • Audio quality check required

Smart Mode Selection

  • Auto-recommend based on quality
  • Prevent precision for good audio
  • Force downgrade at limits
  • Show cost implications

Usage Monitoring

  • Daily cost alerts
  • Anomaly detection
  • Manual review triggers
  • Automatic throttling
